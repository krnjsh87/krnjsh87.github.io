TESTING PHILOSOPHY

My approach combines structured test design with exploratory testing to uncover issues that scripted tests may miss.
I focus on real‑world usage patterns, data consistency across systems, and edge cases around time zones, offline workflows, and integrations.

Offline Sync Issue

In a workforce management product, I identified a defect where offline mobile actions were overriding centralized timesheet data after sync.
By reproducing the conflict across multiple offline/online scenarios and devices, I isolated the conditions, worked with developers to redesign the sync logic, and prevented incorrect attendance and payroll records for field staff.

During rollout of location‑level Daylight Saving Time configuration, I discovered that certain combinations of settings caused time shifts and inconsistent reporting.
