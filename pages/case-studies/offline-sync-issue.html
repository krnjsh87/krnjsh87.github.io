Case Study: Offline Sync Issue

Context: Workforce management product with offline mobile usage.

Problem: Offline actions from mobile overwrote centralized timesheet records after synchronization.

Approach: Reproduced conflicts across device/network scenarios, isolated collision conditions, and validated revised sync logic.

Outcome: Prevented incorrect attendance and payroll records for field teams.
